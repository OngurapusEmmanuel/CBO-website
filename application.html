Join Our Team

At Bethel Mental Wellbeing, we are dedicated to improving lives through compassionate care and innovative solutions. Join us in making a difference!

Current Opportunities

Physiotherapists (Interns)

We are seeking passionate physiotherapy interns to assist in providing physical therapy services, including assessment, treatment planning, and rehabilitation for clients with diverse needs. This role offers an opportunity to work closely with experienced professionals in a supportive environment.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in evaluating client physical conditions and developing treatment plans.
  • Provide therapeutic exercises and interventions under supervision.
  • Document client progress and communicate effectively with the care team.

Requirements: Enrolled in or recently completed a physiotherapy program.

Location: Nairobi, Kenya

Occupational Therapists (Interns)

Join our team as an occupational therapy intern to support clients in regaining independence and improving their quality of life. This role involves hands-on experience in delivering client-centered therapy.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in conducting assessments to identify client needs and goals.
  • Develop and implement therapeutic activities to enhance daily living skills.
  • Collaborate with families and caregivers to support client progress.

Requirements: Enrolled in or recently completed an occupational therapy program.

Location: Nairobi, Kenya

Counsellors (Interns)

We are looking for counseling interns to provide emotional support, guidance, and intervention to clients. This role is ideal for those seeking to make a meaningful impact in the field of mental health.

Responsibilities:

  • Conduct initial client assessments and identify areas of concern.
  • Provide individual and group counseling sessions under supervision.
  • Maintain accurate records and collaborate with the care team.

Requirements: Enrolled in or recently completed a counseling or psychology program.

Location: Nairobi, Kenya

Speech Development Specialists (Interns)

Assist clients in developing and improving their speech and communication skills. This role provides valuable experience for those pursuing a career in speech-language pathology.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in conducting speech and language assessments.
  • Develop and implement individualized therapy plans.
  • Monitor client progress and adjust interventions as needed.

Requirements: Enrolled in or recently completed a speech-language pathology program.

Location: Nairobi, Kenya

How to Apply

If you are interested in any of the above positions, please send your CV and a cover letter to careers@bethelmentalwellbeing.org. Ensure to include the position title in the subject line of your email.

Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.

We look forward to hearing from you!
