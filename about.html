About

History

Bethel Mental Wellbeing is a Community Based Organisation in Kakamega county, Kenya. It was founded in 2023 doing mental health education among members of Hossana Community Church then registered in 2024.

It is an organisation that focuses on mental wellbeing of everyone in the community and persons with mental disabilities. It does mental health awareness and sensitisation in work places, school, Baraza's and in any platform given opportunity.

Our core services include counselling, psychosocial support, support groups, door to door services such as physiotherapy, occupational therapy and speech development therapy, with the main obejective of achieving the sustainable goal by 2030 for both persons with mental disabilities or without any disabilities. It advocates for mental wellness as it deconstruct myths and beliefs of mentally challenged persons.

Our Objectives

To curb new cases of mentally challenged newborns and persons
To create awareness and sensitisation of the importance of mental health
To give counselling services and psychosocial support to persons within the community
To advocate for inclusion of a rights-based approach in workplaces to reduce inequality
To promote formal and informal education, vocational training, and life skills to mentally challenged persons
To create decent work and economic growth among everyone in the community through support groups to reduce poverty
To advocate and promote good health and wellbeing among mentally challenged persons
To create, promote, and link mentally challenged persons to talents and sports of their choice and specialty
