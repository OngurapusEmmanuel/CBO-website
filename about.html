About Us

Bethel Intellectual Disability Centre (BID-C) is a Community Based Organisation (CBO) that was established in 2023 in western Kenya with an objective to provide health care,education and economic empowerment through giving health care serices and education to the intellectually disabled.

History

It began in 2023 doing sensitisation in Western Kenya, Kakamega county in Marama North Ward giving caregivers and parents psychosocial support because of being looked down upon and isolated by people in the community because of being seen as outcursed to the community.

In Western Kenya people with intellectual disabilities or any form of disability are abandoned and are viewed as outcursed. People would isolate them and fear to associate of or help them in any way. Parents and family to such persons are seen as people who are in cult worshipping and used their children or child to gain wealth. Others think their their parents or family members did something that is a taboo or things that are against the norm of the community. This contributed to people hiding such people in their homestead in an uncommon areas in the homestead that is why we saw the need to come and solve this community challenge. This C.B.O is under a church by the name Hossana Community Church Centre.

BID-C being a C.B.O for persons with intellectual disabilities has helped people to accept people with mental disabilities and connect them to places or organisations to help them lead a healthy life and economically empowered through education and trainings as compared to being abandoned in their homestead.
