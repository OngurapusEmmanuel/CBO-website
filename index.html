About Us

Bethel Mental Welleing is a Community Based Organisation (CBO) that was established in 2023 in western Kenya with an objective to put peoples' mental health forefront. It also focuses on every person in the community that needs conselling, support groups counselling and psychosocial support involving girl talk, boy talk and teen talk to have good mental wellbeing in the commnity.

Mental challenges and disability is pronounced and has led to exclusion and discrimination of persons with such challenges in the society.

Our Impact

Get to know our impact in society

Bethel Mental Wellbeing is a community-based organization dedicated to advocating for mental health and wellbeing in Kakamega. We strive to empower local communities by ensuring access to key Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by 2030, including quality education, healthcare, employment opportunities, and inclusivity for all individuals, particularly those with mental challenges. Our initiatives focus on promoting gender equality and equity, ensuring that every person, regardless of their mental health status, has the opportunity to thrive and contribute to society.
