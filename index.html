Who we are

About Us

Bethel Intellectual Disability Centre (BID-C) is a Community Based Organisation (CBO) that was established in 2023 in western Kenya with an objective to provide health care,education and economic empowerment through giving health care serices and education to the mentally & intellectually disabled.

Mental and intellectual disabilities is pronounced and has led to exclusion and discrimination of persons with such disabilities in the society.

Our Mission

To give a positive impact on lives of persons to be independent and contribute to the growth of income of the country.
