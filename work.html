Internships

In line with our vision and corporate responsibility, we offer internship opportunities for talented individuals who have recently completed their undergraduate studies.

Periodically, these internship placements are open to the public so as to attract talent. The nature and duration of the internship will vary from individual to individual and will be stipulated in an engagement contract or Internâ€™s Application Form.

A well-defined internship program with clear deliverables is in place and successful interns are exposed to all of our business units i.e. Strategic Leadership, Financial Capability, and Operations Management under the supervision of highly experienced personnel.

To develop them further, each intern is required to come up with a project that addresses a pertinent issue at Fatmak as their immediate client. With exceptional performance, this results in an eventual placement with the organization.

Always lookout for these opportunities on our website: www.bidc.com
